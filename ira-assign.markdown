IRQ | Assignment
----|-------------
0   | System Timer
1   | Keyboard
2   | Cascaded with IRQ 9
3   | Default COM2 and COM4
4   | Default COM1 and COM3
5   | LPT2
6   | Floppy Drive Controller
7   | LPT1
8   | Real Time Clock
9   | SEE 2
10  | Open
11  | Open
12  | PS/2 mouse or Open
13  | Math Coprocessor
14  | Primary Hard Drive controller
15  | Secondary Hard Drive controller
